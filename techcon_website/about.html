Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots effort to connect local developers, innovators, and tech leaders. Over the years, it has grown into an international event attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech industry. Our mission is to empower developers, entrepreneurs, and creators by providing access to the latest tools, insights, and networking opportunities.
